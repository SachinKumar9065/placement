Bar Ghaph of AKTU

The bar graph representing year-wise placements in AKTU (Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University) illustrates the number of placements for each year. The graph consists of a horizontal axis representing the years and a vertical axis representing the number of placements. Each year is assigned a bar whose height corresponds to the number of placements achieved in that particular year. This visual representation allows viewers to easily compare and analyze the placement trends over time. It helps identify any significant increases, decreases, or patterns in placements from year to year, aiding stakeholders in assessing the effectiveness of the university's placement efforts.

Pie Chart of AKTU

The pie chart representing year-wise placements in AKTU (Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University) showcases the distribution of placements across different years. Each slice of the pie corresponds to a specific year, and its size represents the proportion of placements for that year compared to the total number of placements. The pie chart provides a visual overview of the relative contribution of each year to the overall placements. It allows viewers to identify any significant variations or trends in placements over time. This representation helps stakeholders, such as students, faculty, and recruiters, gain insights into the annual performance of placements and make informed decisions based on the distribution of opportunities.
